The problem

You already know what should be automated. You just never have time to do it.

Context is scattered

Slack threads, Notion docs, Jira tickets, emails — your task context lives everywhere except where you're working.

Repetition goes unresolved

Every executor has a graveyard of tasks they meant to automate. Someday never comes.

Collaboration is friction

Getting a quick answer means pulling someone into a new tool, a new thread, a new context switch.

How it works

The Focus Loop

Four steps. Zero extra work from you.

1

Watch

Focus observes your actions as you work, passively, in the background.

2

Capture

Resources, conversations, and context are automatically gathered around the task.

3

Recognise

Intent is identified. The same pattern surfaces when it detects matching work ahead.

4

Execute

The workflow runs. You approve or it runs silently.

Collaboration

No new tools. No context switches.

Focus meets your team where they already are. When it needs a quick answer, it reaches out in Slack — not another dashboard, not another login. Your colleague replies in the thread. Focus picks it up and keeps going.
